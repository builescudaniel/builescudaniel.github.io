Frequently Asked Questions:
Q: How can I enable notifications for daily football predictions? A: Go to your device's settings, find FootyPredict, and enable notifications from there. Once enabled, you will receive daily notifications when new predictions are available.
Q: Why are there no predictions for today? A: Predictions are updated daily. If there are no predictions available, it may be due to a temporary issue. Please check back later or try refreshing the app.
Q: Can I view predictions for dates other than today? A: Yes, you can view predictions for the last five days using the date picker in the app.
Q: How are the predictions ranked? A: Predictions are ranked by confidence levels, with the first match having the highest confidence.
